Research

Neuroengineering. Medical Devices and Robotics.

Redefining Abilities, One Difference at a Time.

Individuals with disabilities are frequently limited in their ability to perform everyday. I dedicate my efforts to researching and designing innovative solutions aimed at addressing these challenges, specifically enhancing vision and restoring upper limb functionality. I create prosthetic devices that make a real difference in people's lives by blending tech advancements with a solid grasp of how the human body functions. My goal is to provide practical, efficient solutions that noticeably enhance the quality of life for those who rely on them.

Unraveling the Mysteries of the Human Brain.

The limited extent of our knowledge concerning the human brain is striking, considering its integral role in governing our actions. I am deeply intrigued by the intricacies of the brain and am motivated to delve deeper into understanding it. Specifically, I am keenly interested in exploring the mechanisms that underlie both motor function and sensory perception. My aim is to utilize this understanding in the enhancement and refinement of neural prostheses.
